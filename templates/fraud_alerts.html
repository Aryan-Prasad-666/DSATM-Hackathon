Fraud Awareness Alerts

Common Scams to Watch Out For

- Never share your bank account number or PIN with anyone who calls you.
- Be careful of messages offering free loans or money; they might be fake.
- Do not give your OTP (one-time password) to anyone, even if they say they are from the bank.
- Avoid clicking links in messages asking for your personal details.
- If someone asks for money to "activate" a loan, it is a trick.

What to Do if You See Something Strange

- Tell your local bank or a trusted person right away.
- Write down the phone number or message you received.
- Do not send money until you are sure it is safe.
- Visit your bank branch to check your account.
